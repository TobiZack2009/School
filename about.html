ABOUT JERARE SUMMIT

A citadel of world class learning.

‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ㅤWelcome to Jerare Summit Secondary School, nestled at 13, Adenekan Salako Close, Ogba, Lagos—a center of learning excellence and discipline. Since our establishment in January 1994, we have been a full-fledged co-educational institution, embracing both Junior and Senior Secondary education levels. Our strategic location makes us easily accessible to students from diverse neighborhoods including Agege, Ikeja, Berger, Oke-Ira, Ifako, Oju, and Agidingbi.

‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ Founded by the visionary educationist, Mrs. Folashade Ojutalayo, our school has become synonymous with quality education and academic distinction. In 1997, our commitment to educational excellence was recognized by the Lagos State Government, granting us full approval as a distinguished private secondary school within the state.

‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ㅤOur accreditation journey continued with the West African Examinations Council in 1998 and the National Examinations Council in 2000, further establishing Jerare Summit as a reputable center for external examinations such as SSCE (WAEC & NECO) and BECE. For over two decades, we have been at the forefront of academic rigor and success.

‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ㅤAt Jerare Summit, we pride ourselves on providing a secure learning environment upheld by a high standard of discipline. Our team of experienced and dedicated teachers is committed to nurturing and guiding our students towards achieving their full potential. Join us, and be part of a tradition of excellence that shapes the leaders of tomorrow.

Why Choose Us?

Academic Excellence

Since our inception, we have been presenting candidates for external examinations such as the Senior Secondary Certificate Examination-West African Examinations Council (SSCE-WACE), National Examinations Council (NECO), and Junior Secondary Certificate Examination (JSCE). Our curriculum is comprehensive, changing, and designed to meet international standards.

Holistic Education

Understanding the multifaceted nature of education, we place equal emphasis on non-academic pursuits. Our extracurricular activities are diverse and include sports, music, drama, debate, community service, and various clubs and societies. These activities are integral to our curriculum and are designed to foster teamwork, leadership, and social responsibility among our students. Our experienced and dedicated faculty members are committed to providing an environment that is nurturing, yet challenging. They employ innovative teaching methods and technology to engage students and facilitate a deeper understanding of the subject matter.

Facilities and Resources

Our campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, science laboratories, a library with an extensive collection of resources, computer labs, and recreational areas. These facilities provide an ideal learning environment that stimulates intellectual curiosity and supports a wide range of educational activities.
HISTORY

2000

The National Examinations Council (NECO) grants approval to the school. The school becomes a center for external examinations: SSCE (WAEC & NECO) and BECE.

1998

The West African Examinations Council (WAEC) approves Jerare Summit Secondary School as a center for external examinations.

1997

The school receives approval from the Lagos State Government as a recognized private secondary school.

1994

Jerare Summit Secondary School is established as a full-fledged co-educational secondary school.
