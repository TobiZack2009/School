ABOUT Jerare Summit

A citadel of world class learning.

Jerare Summit Secondary School was established in 1994 and was approved by the Lagos State Government in 1998. It is both a day and mixed school. The school has been presenting candidates for external examinations SSCE-WACE, NECO and JSCE. Education is the bedrock upon which the growth of a nation stands. Giving quality education with mental, social and moral values remains our commitment. These, we believe will help in building a dynamic, united and an enterprising society particularly in a union with diverse cultural backgrounds.
